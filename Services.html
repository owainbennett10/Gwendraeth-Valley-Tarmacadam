Our Services

Gwendraeth Valley tarmacadam offers a wide range of services. We have very experienced staff and we provide seamless delivery of services around the clock to ensure our customers have a high standard of service. If you have any questions please contact us.

  • Tarmacadam Roads

    With our ever-improving equipment and our highly skilled staff we at Gwendraeth Valley Tarmac work closely with our clients from trunk roads to farm lanes.

  • Tarmacing Drives and Car Parks

    If you want your driveway laid with tarmac, call the experts at Gwendraeth Valley Tarmacadam. Our services are available in Carmarthenshire.

  • Machinery Hire

    In addition to tarmacing services, we can also hire out paver and roller machinery. Feel free to contact us if you want to hire machinery from us.

  • Ground Works

    At Gwendraeth Valley Tarmacadam, we offer comprehensive groundwork services for our clients. From initial site surveys to completion, we will carry out all aspects of groundwork; contact us now.

  • Resin Surfacing

    With resin surfacing, your driveway, patio, or swimming pool surrounds will get a beautiful and long-lasting surface. We offer different types of resin-bound surfacing at highly affordable prices. One of the most important features of a resin surface is its anti-slip nature. Call us if you need our resin surfacing services in the area.

  • Customised Resin surfacing

    As we offer our resin surfacing work in a wide range of colors and textures, we can lay a surface that is personalized according to your requirements. Our experts will ensure that all services are carried out to the highest standards.
